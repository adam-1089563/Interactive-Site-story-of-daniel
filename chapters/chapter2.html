Chapter 2: The Diagnosis

Doctor: (calmly) “Daniel has a chronic condition that affects his immune system. It will require regular treatments, and we’ll need to monitor him closely.”

Maria: (voice trembling) “How… how much will this cost?”

Doctor: “There are programs that can help, and I’ll connect you to a social worker. But it will be a journey.”

Daniel: (whispering) “Am I going to be okay?”

Jorge: (firmly) “Yes, son. We’ll make sure of it. You just focus on being strong.”
