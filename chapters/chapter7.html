Chapter 7: Scholarship Announcement

Several years later

The family’s apartment. Daniel holds a letter while Maria and Jorge look on nervously.

Daniel: (reading) “Congratulations! You’ve been awarded a full scholarship to State University.”

Maria: (bursting into tears) “I knew you could do it, mijo!”

Jorge: (hugging Daniel) “Your hard work paid off. We’re so proud of you.”

Lily: (teasing) “Does this mean you’re buying me a car one day?”

Daniel: (laughing) “One step at a time, Lily.”
