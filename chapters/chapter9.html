Chapter 9: The Turning Point

A community center, where Daniel speaks to a group of children.

Daniel: “I was just like you, growing up in a small apartment, wondering if I could ever dream big. But I had people who believed in me and pushed me to keep going. And now, I’m here to tell you—your dreams are possible too.”

Young Boy: “Do you really think I can go to college one day?”

Daniel: (kneeling to his level) “Not only can you, but I’ll be here to help you get there. Deal?”

Young Boy: (smiling) “Deal!”
